Top five sheep breeds:

1. Merino The merino sheep is an extremely popular sheep breed, originating in Spain it is known for its fine fiber that is much thinner than human hair. Merino sheep originated in the flocks of the spanish elite, they were bred bor both wool and meat, they live best in reletivly arid climates and were exported to New Zeland where they are now the most common sheep breed.

2. licheser Long Wool The lichester longwool is a british sheep breed from the midlands, it was desigened in the 1700s by Robert Blackwell, a proponent for modern animal breeding. The lichester longwool was created in a effort to make the ideal wool breed. today it is very rare with only 500 sheep in all of england.

3.Dorset Horn The dorset Horn is another british sheep breed, it is the oly sheep breed that cns give birth in winter, and is known for bieng able to give birth two times a year insted of once. Sadly the breed is endangered and is in bieng rehabilitated in the U.K,New Zeland, Australia and the U.S.A.

4. Turcana The Turcana sheep is a Romainan sheep breed bred for life in th high altitudes of the carpathian mountains. It is also very common in other parts of the balkains.

5. Dorper The dorper sheep is a South African sheep breed. it is bred for meat and milk, it doesn't produce wool but in it's case that is an advantage because it was raised to live in the most arid and hot deserts.
